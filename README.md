# People Analytics Dashboard

An end-to-end people analytics project analyzing employee lifecycle data 
including attrition, performance, compensation, satisfaction, and engagement 
across 1,470 employee records and 8 departments.

## Workforce Summary
- **Total Employees:** 1,470
- **Attrition Rate:** 17.96%
- **Average Salary:** $123,394
- **Average Tenure:** 7.3 years
- **Average Satisfaction:** 3.01/5.0
- **Average Engagement:** 3.00/5.0

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate: 17.96%
- Early tenure attrition (<2 yrs) is significantly higher
- Compensation and Better Opportunity are top attrition drivers
- Outstanding performers show lower attrition rates

## Dataset Features
- 1,470 employee records
- 8 departments
- 7 job levels (Junior to VP)
- 6 US locations
- Performance ratings, satisfaction, engagement scores
- Attrition reasons and tenure data
